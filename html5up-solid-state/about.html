Why Lambda?

Hi, my name is Alec and I am a student at Lambda School. Prior to Lambda School I was previously enrolled in community college
pre-computer science courses. During school I was not enjoying my classes, and I also found it difficult to reach out for help. After almost
3 years of on and off classes and switching paths I discovered an ad for Lambda. At first I was skeptical when I found out I didn't have to
pay upfront, however after reading some reviews and watching some videos it seemed like the place I wanted to be. I eventually applied and am now a part-time student at Lambda. I can honestly say I love the scene around Lambda a lot more than I did in college.
Now to see where this takes me..
