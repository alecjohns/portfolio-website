Bio

Hi, My name is Alec and I am 20 years old. I am from Southern Illinois and have been here all my life! I have 2 brothers, me being in the middle. My family also has 4 dogs! Growing up I played a lot of sports. My favorites being basketball, track, and football.

How do I spend my time?

When I am not at work or in Lambda hours you can most likely find me riding my motorcycle. (As long as its nice out). The bike pictured was my first street-legal bike. I have been riding on two wheels since I was about 3. To me it is easier than driving a car. Throughout my life I have owned 9 different bikes (including dirt bikes). However now I sadly only own 2.

My Main Hobby

Though I love motorcycles it is not always optimal for riding. I tend to spend this time working with computers. Whether it is griding the ranks of league of legends, to putting together a new build. This is my newest and favorite build I have done. This is also my current every-day-use pc. It is powered by a Ryzen 7 2700x processor with a GeForce RTX 2060 Graphics Card. It also houses 16gb of ram with an Aorus b450 Motherboard. And last but not least a 750w power supply. Not the beefiest build but it can handle what I need it to!
